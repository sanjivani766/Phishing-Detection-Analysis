# Phishing Detection & Awareness System Report

## 📌 Project Overview
This project demonstrates the identification, analysis, and awareness of phishing email attacks.

The objective was to analyze phishing email samples, identify attack indicators, classify risks, and provide prevention guidelines.


## 📧 Phishing Emails Analyzed
- Sample Email
- PayPal Phishing Email Example
- Banking Phishing Email Example

## 🚩 Phishing Indicators Identified
- Suspicious sender email address
- Urgent or threatening language
- Fake login links
- Domain spoofing
- Generic greetings
- Grammar and spelling errors


## ⚠ Risk Classification
- Credential Theft – High Risk
- Financial Fraud – High Risk
- Malware Delivery – Medium Risk
- Data Exposure – Medium Risk

## 🛡 Prevention Guidelines
- Verify sender domain before responding
- Do not click unknown links
- Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
- Report suspicious emails to IT team
- Check URLs before entering credentials


## 🧪 Tools Used
- Manual Email Analysis
- Email Header Inspection
- VirusTotal (for URL scanning)
- Online Header Analyzer
